# Sprint 1 Retrospective 

## What went well
- Everyone worked effectively on their issues
- There was clear communication all throughout, whether in person or online.
- There were not much issues that came up regarding the project or teamwork, everything seems to flow nicely and we don't get much in the way of eachother.

## What did not go well
- Our estimation of issue weights were not accurate, we had the tendency to underestimate the efforts needed to complete certain issues. 

## Things to change in next sprint 
- Better estimation of issue weights
- Diversify the type of issues each teammates work on, rather than having one person work on many issues for one thing.
